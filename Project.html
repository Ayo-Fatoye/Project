Welcome to SENT

A website where Nigerian students are enlightened on how to prepare themselves to study in the US.

I made this website during my preparations to apply to schools in the U.S. I must say it isn't roses for someone who has inadequate information. It can be very tasking from making your college list to accepting your admission letter. I know not everyone has the luxury of consulting private organizations to help them so this website is here to guide and help you all the way. Nigerian students who are already in the U.S will also assist you, all for free!
